07 August 2021

Urumasi updated:

  • Cryogenic storage now applies stasis to stop your metabolism.
  • You no longer snore while in deep cryogenic sleep.

09 July 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • map export now works and can be used to save an area of the map
  • Transit tube dispenser stations are now buildable via RPD
  • Transit tube dispenser station sprite errors have been fixed. The sprites themselves have not been changed, but the names and orientation has been corrected.

01 July 2021

MaltVinegar updated:

  • increases the RCL's capacity to 900

29 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Turbines no longer produce ridiculous power and throw tons of runtimes when the intake is a wall
  • Conveyor switches can be toggled between two-way and one-way with a screwdriver when in item form
  • Conveyor switches remember their one-way state when turned into item form
  • You can now use the Activate Held Object and Drop Object hotkeys (default Z and Q, respectively) to activate arm-implanted tools and put objects away. You must have the hand free before you can activate it this way.
  • Arm implant tools are no longer no-drop, and instead simply snap back if they get dropped. Don't sweat it, if you slip just press Z again.

Urumasi updated:

  • You can now carefully walk over piles of items to avoid tripping over them.
  • We have recalibrated recyclers to stop them from eating your hardsuit helmet lights.
  • Nanotrasen apologizes for the shipment of defective deepcore pinpointers and sent a new one on the house.

28 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Gentle slime crossbreeds can be made with pink extracts. They can be used to emulate luminescent activations of extracts.

24 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • All constructors have a universal right-click "Toggle ore silo" verb, notably the plumbing and light constructors

23 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Shuttle mass calculation

22 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • radial menus now ignore the anchor's current alpha, color and transform.

21 June 2021

Urumasi updated:

  • Ore vein pinpointers now correctly only show ores that are on your z-level.

13 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Direcitonal firelocks no longer block the turf they're on from all directions when closed

Urumasi updated:

  • Fixed a bug where the RCD could be used for free

09 June 2021

KubeRoot updated:

  • Clicking airlock electronics with another airlock electronics will now copy access settings to the one you clicked on
  • Unpowered cryosleepers can be spawned from.
  • Destroyed cryosleepers are removed as possible spawn locations.
  • Construction bags can now hold sheets, rods and floor tiles
  • Construction bags have greatly increased capacity
  • Departmental protolathe and techfab boards are now printable.
  • Circuit Imprinter boards print departmental version now.
  • Add construction bags to Robco vending machines, available roundstart
  • Smoke machines can now be rotated via alt-click or right-click verbs
  • Removed the old autolathe UI
  • TGUI autolathe UI
  • Multiple users can browse autolathe UI without seeing the same tabs.
  • You can now eject materials from an autolathe
  • You can now queue items in an autolathe
  • You can now toggle continuous fabrication mode on an autolathe queue, or an item in the autolathe queue meaning it will build repeatedly until the autolathe runs out of materials.
  • Trigger build wire on autolathes
  • Autolathe queue
  • Autolathe ejection direction
  • Autolathe nolonger opens when something is printed
  • Removed build button from autolathe's UI.
  • You cannot go over 50 and under 0 with "+" and "-" buttons in autolathe's UI.
  • Cable coils can be recolored via a right-click verb
  • Cable coil stack size increased from 30 to 300
  • Stock parts now use BeeStation sprites where applicable
  • Added BeeStation stock parts and power cells by SomeAngryMiner and Dingo-Dongler
  • Advanced Multitool, which can store 6 machines for linking purposes
